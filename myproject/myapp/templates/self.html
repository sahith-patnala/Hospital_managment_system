Self Assesment Tools

What is self assesment tools?

Self-assessment tools for mental health are questionnaires or tests that you can take yourself to screen for symptoms of mental health conditions. They are not a substitute for professional diagnosis or treatment, but they can be a helpful first step in understanding your mental health.
