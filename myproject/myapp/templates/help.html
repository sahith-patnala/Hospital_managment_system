Help & Support

Welcome to our Help & Support page. Here you will find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use our platform effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage and fill in your details.
  • How can I book a virtual counseling session?
    Once logged in, navigate to the "Counseling" section and select a suitable time slot with an available counselor.
  • Are my personal details safe?
    Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security with robust data protection measures to ensure your information remains confidential.
  • How do I use the self-assessment tools?
    Go to the "Self-Assessment" section, choose the appropriate tool, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the assessment.
